# Period Ozone Trend Summary

## Questions

- Was depletion faster before 1987? Yes. Period 1 global slope is -2.0372 DU/year; the mean post-1987 slope across periods 2-4 is -0.2790 DU/year.
- Is recovery visible afterward? Yes. Recovery is counted where a later period has a positive global-mean slope.

## Period Slopes

- Period 1: 1979-1987: -2.0372 DU/year. Depletion-dominated period: the global grid-cell mean trend is negative.
- Period 2: 1988-2000: -1.1008 DU/year. Depletion-dominated period: the global grid-cell mean trend is negative.
- Period 3: 2001-2010: -0.4254 DU/year. Depletion-dominated period: the global grid-cell mean trend is negative.
- Period 4: 2011-2020: 0.6892 DU/year. Recovery-dominated period: the global grid-cell mean trend is positive.
- Period 5: 2021-Present: n/a. No gridded data are available in this workspace for this period.

## Data Note

The local gridded satellite files available here cover 1978-2020. No gridded 2021-present ozone files were found, so Period 5 is reported as unavailable.
